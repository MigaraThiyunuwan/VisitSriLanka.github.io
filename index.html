Welcome To Sri Lanka

featured places

Sigiriya

Sigiriya or Sinhagiri is an ancient rock fortress located in the northern Matale District near the town of Dambulla in the Central Province, Sri Lanka.

Galle

Galle is a city on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka. It is known for Galle Fort, the fortified old city founded by Portuguese colonists in the 16th century.

Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura is a major city in Sri Lanka. It is the capital city of North Central Province, Sri Lanka and the capital of Anuradhapura District.

Temple Of Tooth Relic,Kandy

Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic commonly known as the ශ්‍රී දළදා මාළිගාව, is a Buddhist temple in Kandy, Sri Lanka.

Nelligala Buddhist Center,Kandy

Nelligala International Buddhist Center is a Buddhist temple situated in Muruthalawa village in the Kandy district.

Thiru Koneshwaram Kovil,Trincomalee

The Temple of the Thousand Pillars and Dakshina-Then Kailasam is a classical-medieval Hindu temple complex in Trincomalee

Sinharaja Forest

Sinharaja Forest Reserve is a forest reserve and a biodiversity hotspot in Sri Lanka.

Hikkaduwa

Hikkaduwa is a seaside resort town in southwestern Sri Lanka. It’s known for its strong surf and beaches

Nallur Kovil,Jaffna

Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil is a significant Hindu temple, located in Nallur, Northern Province, Sri Lanka.

Hunnasgiriya,Mathale

Hunnasgiriya is a beautiful village in Sri Lanka. It is located within Central Province.

Kelaniya Temple

The Kelaniya Raja Maha Vihara is a Buddhist temple in Kelaniya, Sri Lanka. It is located 11 km north-east of Colombo.

Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya is a city in the tea country hills of central Sri Lanka.
